Description

LinearOrdinaryDifferentialOperatorRegularSolutions(R, F, , RX, RXD, FX) provides a regular solver for linear ordinary differential operators with polynomial coefficients. Regular solutions are solutions of the form where the are polynomials and the are in the fraction field of R, so they include in particular radical solutions, i.e. solutions such that is a rational function for some .
