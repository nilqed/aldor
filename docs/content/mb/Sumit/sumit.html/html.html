The design and development of was a project of Manuel Bronstein's computer algebra group in the Scientific Computation Institute of the ETH Zurich, Switzerland. Since October 1997, the further development of is being carried on in the CAFÉ project at INRIA Sophia-Antipolis (France). During 2001, an important subset of the library was extracted and incorporated in the Algebra 1library. Besides the principal author, the following ETH and INRIA project members have contributed to the library:
Marco Codutti
Thom Mulders
