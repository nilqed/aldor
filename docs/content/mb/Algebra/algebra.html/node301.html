ModulopGaussElimination


Usage

import from ModulopGaussElimination


Exports

deter: (M, Z, V, Z, Z, Z) Z Determinant
determinant!: (M, Z, Z) Z Determinant
extendedRowEchelon!: (M, Z, Z, Z) (V, Z, V, M) REF of a matrix
firstDependence!: (Generator V, Z, Z, M, M) Z First dependence relation
inverse!: (M, Z, Z, M, V) () Inverse
kernel!: (M, Z, Z, M) Z Kernel
maxInvertibleSubmatrix!: (M, Z, Z) (V, V) Maximal minor
particularSolution!: (M, Z, Z, M, Z, Z, M, V) () A solution
rank!: (M, Z, Z, Z) Z Rank
rowEchelon!: (M, Z, Z, Z) (V, Z, V) REF of a matrix
solve!: (M, Z, Z, M, Z, Z, M, V, M) Z All solutions
span!: (M, Z, Z) V Span


where

Z == MachineInteger
V == PrimitiveArray MachineInteger
M == PrimitiveArray PrimitiveArray MachineInteger


Description

This domain implements ordinary Gaussian elimination on dense matrices over the integers modulo a machine prime.
