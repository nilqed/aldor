PrimeCollection


Usage

PrimeCollection: Category


Description

PrimeCollection is the category of collections of primes with various properties and various sizes.


Exports

allPrimes: () Generator Z Generate all the primes
fourierPrime: Z (Z, Z) Fourier prime
maxPrime: Z Largest prime
nextPrime: Z Z First prime above a given number
previousPrime: Z Z First prime below a given number
primRoot: Z Z Modular primitive root
randomPrime: () Z Random prime


where

Z == MachineInteger
