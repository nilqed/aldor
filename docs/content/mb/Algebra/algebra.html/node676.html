PartiallyOrderedType


Usage

PartiallyOrderedType: Category


Description

PartiallyOrderedType is the category of partially ordered types. Hence, a domain of this category is endowed with a transitive binary relation such that either or does not hold for every x and y. If both do not hold then x and y are called not comparable for . In particular if holds then x and y are not omparable for .
