Exceptions

SALLI can several different exceptions: open throws the exception FileException if the file cannot be opened in the desired mode, accessing a CheckingArray or CheckingMemoryBlock out of bounds throws the exception ArrayException, and calling next! on an empty generator throws the exception GeneratorException. Those exceptions are of type FileExceptionType, ArrayExceptionType and GeneratorExceptionType respectively. No function in SALLI makes a call to error.
